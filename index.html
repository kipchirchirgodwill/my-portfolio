The Early Years

Born in Kapseret, Uasin Gishu

I was born and raised in Kapseret, Uasin Gishu County — a place that shaped who I am. My childhood was filled with joy, laughter, and the warmth of friendships built through the simple pleasure of playing games with other kids. I was always the social one, drawing people in and making memories that still make me smile today.

Primary School

St. George's Primary School

My formal education began at St. George's Primary School, where I laid the foundations that would carry me far. Curious by nature, I was always asking questions and looking at the world with wonder and enthusiasm.

Secondary School

Kebulonik Secondary School

At Kebulonik Secondary School, I had grand ambitions — I dreamed of becoming an aeronautical engineer, fascinated by the skies and the science of flight. But life had a different and equally exciting plan in store for me.

The Turning Point · Form Four

A Passion for Code Is Born

It was during my final year of secondary school that everything changed. I discovered software development — and the idea that you could build anything from just your mind and a keyboard felt like pure magic. From that moment on, I never looked back.

Today

Full Stack Development at ElDoHub, Eldoret

Today, I am sharpening my craft as a Full Stack Software Development student at ElDoHub in Eldoret. I'm building real-world projects — a business website for a client, and HealthBox, an AI-powered wellness companion app. Every day brings me closer to the developer I know I can become.

"I want to own a successful tech company and help the next generation navigate a world driven by technology."

Technology is moving faster than ever, and I refuse to be left behind. My goal is not just to keep up with advances in AI and software — but to use them as tools to solve real problems for real people.

I believe the most powerful thing you can do with knowledge is pass it on. Empowering the younger and upcoming generation in tech is at the very core of everything I am building toward.
